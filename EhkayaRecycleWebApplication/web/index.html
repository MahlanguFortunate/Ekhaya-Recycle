ABOUT US

A development team focused on building practical technology solutions for everyday challenges. The approach combines user-centred design with efficient system development to create reliable and accessible applications. Each solution begins with understanding community needs, followed by purposeful development that supports sustainability and long-term impact.

Our Approach aims the platform connects households with nearby recycling centres through a structured pickup scheduling system. Users create profiles, request collections, and prepare recyclable materials for collection. During pickup, materials are weighed on-site and the recorded information is captured within the system for transparency and tracking.

After processing at the recycling centre, the calculated value is credited to a digital wallet linked to the user profile. Recycling data is continuously analysed to provide insights into individual and community contributions, promoting awareness and encouraging sustainable recycling habits.
